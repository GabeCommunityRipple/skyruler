How it works

From address to measurements in minutes

STEP 01
Enter the address
Type in any US property address. The tool loads a satellite view instantly — no app or download needed.
STEP 02
Draw the zones
Click to trace the lawn, driveway, lot, or fence line on the satellite image. Square footage calculates instantly.
STEP 03
Generate report
One click builds a branded image with the satellite photo, measurements, logo, and optional price estimate.
STEP 04
Send the quote
Text or email the branded report straight to the contractor. They have measurements before leaving the office.
